ABOUT DE-RAHEEMS CONCEPTS

The company, ‘DE RAHEEMS CONCEPTS LIMITED’ was established in 2008 and fully incorporated in 2013 with the sole aim of providing professional and other human developmental services to government, individual and private organizations.
It has offices in Kaduna and Abuja, Nigeria.

In her quest to address some of the long-standing environmental and infrastructural challenges faced in most Nigerian societies, the company for the over five years has been offering value-oriented voluntary and consulting services in:

  • Drainage Constructions
  • Buildings and Renovations
  • Solid Waste Collection and Disposal Management
  • Pre-Drilling & Geophysical Surveys
  • Borehole Drillings for individuals, communities and private bodies across Kaduna and Abuja
  • General Contracts

Our competent professionals and associates are distinguished by their technical expertise combined with years of experiences thereby ensuring clients effective service and satisfaction.
